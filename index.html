voxify is a reliable and secure web-based speech-to-text tool that enables you to quickly and accurately transcribe your audio and video recordings, as well as dictate your notes instead of typing, saving you time and effort. With features like voice commands for punctuation and formatting, automatic capitalization, and easy import/export options, voxify provides an efficient and user-friendly dictation and transcription experience.

If you use a suitable microphone, the internet will be of good quality and in a quiet place, and you will be able to talk at a good speed and quality, and the accuracy will be above 98%. For audio files, the proximity of the microphone to the speaker and the absence of noise and additional sounds have an effect on the quality of the conversion.

To do voice typing, voxify must first receive your voice. This problem can be due to a broken or weak microphone, incorrect settings of the sound card, or not allowing the browser to receive sound. Read the guide to fix this problem.

Consider the conversion of voice to text as a voice conversation, the low speed of the Internet, the presence of noise in the voice, and the interruption of the Internet interfere with the sending and processing of the voice by the software. In this case, try to test with another internet.
